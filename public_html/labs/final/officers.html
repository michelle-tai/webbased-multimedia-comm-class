Welcome!

Welcome to the Alpha Beta Chapter of alpha Kappa Delta Phi at Duke University. We are Duke's first and only Asian-American interest sorority, and while we are an Asian-interest organization, we're not Asian-exlusive. Our sisters may come from all corners of the world, but we all share commons goals of sisterhood, leadership, scholarship, service, and Asian-Awareness. Feel free to explore this site, our Facebook, and our Instagram to learn more about aKDPhi and our diverse sisters!
