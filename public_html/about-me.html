About Me

I stand at intersections: of Asian and American, of woman and STEM, of art and technology. After struggling for so long to adapt a singular identity, I’ve finally realized that only one thing defines me: Michelle Tai. I’m now more comfortable exploring all aspects of me, whether it be learning about my Taiwanese and Chinese descent, building and programming robots as an Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science double major, or dabbling in photography as the Feature Photo Editor at the Chronicle. I aim to incorporate innovation and empathy in all I do, and I hope to be able to use my skills to help animals in the future.
